Volunteer Opportunities

We are always looking for volunteers to help us with our mission. If you are interested in volunteering, please fill out the form below and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Beach Cleanup

Volunteers collect and analyze ocean waste, promote sustainable practices, and participate in beach and underwater cleanups.

Ocean Cleanup & Plastic Pollution Prevention

4-6 hours per day

Duration: 1 - 12 weeks

Volunteers : aged 16+ and families

Marine Wildlife Rescue and Rehabilitation Program

Participants assist in rescuing, treating, and releasing injured marine animals like sea turtles, dolphins, and seals.

Marine Life Conservation

4-6 hours per day

Duration: 2 - 8 weeks

Volunteers : aged 16+ and families

Coral Reef Conservation & Restoration Program

Volunteers help restore damaged coral reefs by planting coral fragments, monitoring reef health, and raising awareness about marine conservation.

Coral Reef Conservation

5-8 hours per day

Duration: 2 - 12 weeks

Volunteers : aged 18+

Blue Ventures

Volunteers work with marine biologists to study fish populations, monitor ecosystems, and promote sustainable fishing practices.

Sustainable Fisheries & Marine Research

10 - 12 hours per day

Duration: 4- 16 weeks

Volunteers : aged 18+
